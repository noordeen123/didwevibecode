We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to a community where humans and hallucinating LLMs can coexist in a state of mutual misunderstanding.

* Blaming the AI for your own syntax errors.
* Leaving passive-aggressive comments in the codebase addressed to GitHub Copilot.
* Merging straight to `main` because "the vibes were right."
